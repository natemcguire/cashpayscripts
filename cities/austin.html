Visiting Austin or a Student at UT?

If you're in town for SXSW, ACL, a conference, or just a weekend trip — or you're a UT Austin student — you can skip the clinic and get quick access to common medications online. Our doctors review your request and, if appropriate, send your prescription to a nearby pharmacy in just hours.

What Can You Get?

We offer fast, no-hassle treatment for things like coughs, flu, birth control, yeast infections, acne, anxiety, and more. It's a safe, affordable option when you don't have time for a doctor visit or don't want to wait.
